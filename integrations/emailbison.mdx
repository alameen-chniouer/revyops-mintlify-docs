---
title: EmailBison
description: Connect EmailBison to RevyOps via webhooks.
---

## Prerequisites

- An active EmailBison account
- Your RevyOps `client_id` (find it under **Settings → Workspace**)

## Webhook Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open EmailBison Settings">
    In EmailBison, navigate to **Settings → Webhooks**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Webhook URL">
    Enter:

    ```
    https://api.revyops.com/public/emailbison-webhook/{client_id}
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Events">
    Enable all email event types: sent, reply, bounce, unsubscribe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Verify">
    Save and trigger a test event to confirm data arrives in RevyOps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Agency Setup (Global Webhook)

```
https://api.revyops.com/public/global-emailbison-webhook/{agency_id}
```
